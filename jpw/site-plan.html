Content

Home page

Hi, my name is Jesus Paredes (JP Walls) and I am a CMM programmer and fixture designer with almost six years of experience in the field. I am the proud owner of JP Walls, which specializes in offering metrology services, specifically in CMM, as well as building fixtures for various industries, with a focus on aerospace, automotive, and medical.

Images for the Home page

CMM Programming

As a skilled CMM programmer with almost 6 years of experience in the field, I am confident in my ability to provide exceptional metrology services for your business. Our company's philosophy is built around ensuring that our clients receive the highest level of service possible. We use the latest technology and techniques to ensure that our measurements are accurate and reliable, and we pride ourselves on our ability to find innovative solutions to complex problems.

Images for CMM Programming page

Fixture Design

As a fixture designer, my role is to design and create fixtures that are used to hold and position parts during the measurement process. Fixtures are critical components of a successful metrology program, as they ensure that parts are held securely and in the correct position for measurement.
